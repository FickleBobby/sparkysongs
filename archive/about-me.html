About me

I was a street entertainer as a schoolboy, and since my performing arts degree I've been a professional performer and musician with extensive experience in theatre-in-education. For the last 15 years I've specialised in pre-school entertainment, developing my style and material in over 12,000 shows for early-years audiences.
